**Product Type:** Smart Speaker

The Era 300 is Sonos' first speaker designed specifically for spatial audio, featuring a unique shape optimized for Dolby Atmos playback.

- **RAM:** 2 GB

- Spatial audio with Dolby Atmos support
- Six strategically positioned drivers
- Unique design for 360-degree sound
- Amazon Alexa and Sonos Voice Control
- Line-in support (USB-C)
- Trueplay tuning (iOS only)
