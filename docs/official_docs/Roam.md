# Sonos Roam

**Product Type:** Portable Speaker  
**Release Date:** March 9, 2021  
**Price:** $169  
**Operating System:** S2

## Overview

The Roam is Sonos' smallest portable speaker with both Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity.

## Technical Specifications

- **RAM:** 1 GB
- **Storage:** 4 GB
- **WiFi:** 802.11a/b/g/n/ac (Wi-Fi 5), 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz dual-band
- **Bluetooth:** Bluetooth 5.0
- **Battery Life:** Up to 10 hours of continuous playback
- **IP Rating:** IP67 (waterproof and dustproof)

## Key Features

- Automatic Trueplay tuning
- Bluetooth and Wi-Fi connectivity
- USB-C charging
- Wireless charging capable
- Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant
- Ultra-portable design
- Sound Swap feature
